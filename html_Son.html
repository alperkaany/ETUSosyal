1st International Workshop on Plasticity, Damage and Fracture of Engineering Materials

"The aim of the 1st International Workshop on "Plasticity, Damage and Fracture" of Engineering Materials is to discuss recent progress in the fields of plasticity, damage, and fracture mechanics. The workshop is co-organized by the Middle East Technical University and TOBB University of Economics and Technology, and will be held on 22-23 August 2019 in Ankara, Turkey."

Yer: TOBB ETÜ Konferans Salonu
